How is this game played?

Game is played by swaping two pieces of the puzzle. When you first click on some tile, it will be marked, so that you know that you picked that one. Then, when you click another one, those two pieces will swap.

 Do I have to make a profile?

No, it is not required to make a profile for you to be able to play the game. The only downside is that the score will not be measured.

 How do I see my best highscore?

By going to your profile page, you will be able to see your best score, among other data about your profile.

 How do I log out?

By simply clicking logout button, once you log in.
